Beef Tacos Recipe

Here is a delicious Beef Tacos recipe:

Ingredients:

For the Beef Filling:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade: 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp paprika, ½ tsp oregano, ½ tsp onion powder, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt & pepper)
  • ½ cup tomato sauce or salsa
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
For the Tacos:
  • Taco shells (hard or soft tortillas)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey jack, or a blend)
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup guacamole (optional)
  • Salsa (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Lime wedges (optional)

Instructions:

1. Cook the Ground Beef:

  • Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened (about 2-3 minutes).
  • Add the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  • Add the ground beef to the skillet. Break it apart with a spatula and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.

2. Add Seasoning:

  • Once the beef is browned, drain any excess fat if necessary.
  • Stir in the taco seasoning, tomato sauce (or salsa), and water. Mix well to coat the beef evenly.
  • Let the mixture simmer on low heat for 5-7 minutes until the flavors blend and the sauce thickens slightly. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  • Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens.

3. Prepare tortillas:

  • While the beef is simmering, heat the tortillas. You can do this by warming them in a dry skillet over medium heat for 20-30 seconds per side or wrapping them in foil and warming them in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for 10 minutes.

4. Assemble the tacos:

  • Take a warm tortilla and add a spoonful of the seasoned beef mixture to the center.
  • Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese.
  • Add other toppings like sour cream, salsa, jalapeños, and cilantro as desired.
  • Squeeze a lime wedge over the top for a burst of freshness (optional).

5. Serve and enjoy:

  • Serve the beef tacos with extra toppings or sides like refried beans, rice, or guacamole.
